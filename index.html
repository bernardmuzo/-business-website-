About Solid Ink Printing

Your trusted partner for all printing and computer service needs

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Solid Ink Printing Ltd has grown to become one of Zambia's leading providers of printing and computer repair services. What started as a small printing shop has now expanded to offer a comprehensive range of services to both individual and corporate clients.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, affordable printing solutions and reliable computer services with exceptional customer service. We pride ourselves on our 24/7 availability for emergency computer repairs and our quick turnaround times for printing projects.
